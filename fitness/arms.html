ARMS WORKOUT

pushups

  • Gently draw your belly in and tighten your abs as you curl up and forwards slowly
  • Keep your lower back pressed towards the floor throughout the movement
  • beginner: 10rps
  • intermediate: 20rps
  • advanced: 30rps

    • arm circles

  • Start on all fours with wrists directly under shoulder,toes on the floor
  • Press the floor away from you with hands and you should be able to form a straight line
  • beginner: 20rps
  • intermediate: 25rps
  • advanced: 30rps

    • pike pushups

  • move your knees as high as you can
  • Make it easier by moving one knee at a time
  • beginner: 15rps
  • intermediate: 25rps
  • advanced: 35rps

    • burpees

  • Gently draw your belly and tighten your abs as you curl up and froward slowly
  • support your neck with your hands, but dont pull your head
  • beginner: 10rps
  • intermediate: 20rps
  • advanced: 30rps

    • triceps dips

  • touch your toes with your hands at the top of the moment
  • maintain a straight back throughout the movement
  • beginner: 20rps
  • intermediate: 25rps
  • advanced: 30rps
