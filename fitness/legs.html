LEGS WORKOUT

jumping jacks

  • Keep you knees and toes pointing slightly outwards as you jump
  • Land with soft knees on the balls of your feet to protect your knees and go fast as you can without losing control.
  • beginner: 25rps
  • intermediate: 30rps
  • advanced: 35rps

    • lunges

  • Step forward with a leg and bend it until both legs are in a 90 deg angle.
  • push through your front heel to actvate your glutes and get back to the starting position
  • beginner: 20rps
  • intermediate: 25rps
  • advanced: 30rps

    • squats

  • stand with your feet shoulder width apart and your arms stretched forward
  • lower your body until your thighs are parallel with the floor.
  • beginner: 40rps
  • intermediate: 50rps
  • advanced: 60rps

    • leg raises

  • Lie down on your back, and put your hands beneath your hips for support
  • Lift your legs up until they form a right angle with the floor. Slowly brings your legs back down.
  • beginner: 20rps
  • intermediate: 30rps
  • advanced: 40rps

    • wallsit

  • Keep your legs shoulder width apart and a 90 degree angle on your knees.
  • Push your feet towards the floor and your back against the wall.
  • beginner: 15"
  • intermediate: 25"
  • advanced: 35"
